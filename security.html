Security & Privacy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how your data is protected in the Khasi-English Translator App.

🔒 Data Protection

We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore, both of which follow strict industry standards for data protection. Your login credentials and personal data are encrypted and securely stored. Access to data is controlled and regularly monitored.

📄 Privacy Policy

We do not collect unnecessary data. Only information related to translations, favorites, and user preferences is stored to improve your experience. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.

🛡️ Security Features

The app supports secure login via Email/Password, Google Sign-In, and guest mode. Regular updates address security vulnerabilities and ensure data integrity and access control.

👤 User Control

You can delete your translation history, favorites, or account at any time. We give you full control over your stored data.

🧑‍💻 Guest Users

Guest users' data is stored locally on their device using SQLite and is never uploaded to any server.

📊 Data Usage Transparency

We use basic analytics to understand general usage patterns and improve performance. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

🚀 Commitment to Improvement

We regularly review our policies and improve our security practices. Your feedback helps us keep your data safe.

📩 Contact Us

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at support@yourapp.com.
